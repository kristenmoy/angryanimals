Game Title: Angry Animals

Unique Mechanics:
A unique mechanism in my game is animal launching. It is a catapult-like “drag and shoot” mechanism that will launch the animals towards the snakes. The user is able to drag back the animal and it will launch like a projectile into the air. Depending on the force and angle, it may hit a snake and hopefully defeat them. 
The snakes also have blocks surrounding them as “protection”. Since the animals are being launched with force, these blocks are destructible. After a certain amount of destruction, the blocks may break and disappear, leaving the snakes vulnerable. This allows the animals to gain a greater access. There are different strengths to each block. There is the cake block, which has the least amount of health. The metal has a moderate amount of health, and the stone blocks offer the highest protection to the snakes.
Each playable character – the bird, giraffe, and elephant – may have special abilities. The bird does not have any abilities on click, but it flies through the air with less weight. The giraffe has a power boost of 2.5 times the initial speed upon the user clicking the screen. This allows the animal to hit the blocks and enemies at a greater force. The elephant has a slight explosion. This can occur when the user left clicks, or after 0.75 seconds of hitting a destructible block or enemy.
Additionally, once the snakes are defeated, a small explosion animation and sound should play for each one. This shows that the enemy has been defeated. This will occur when the health of the snake reaches 0, or when it falls off the screen.

Layout:
In this game, there are 10 levels that increase in complexity and difficulty.Each level follows a similar structure, where the animals must be launched in order to defeat the snakes. In terms of increasing complexity, there may be more enemies in some levels, enemies with higher health, and stronger blocks for protection. As the levels progress, the player unlocks different animals. Depending on the complexity and difficulty, more tries or more animals may be given to the player to attempt to defeat the snakes. There will be a main menu screen at the beginning that allows the user to see the levels they can play. At the last level, there will be a “final boss” with a larger snake with a large amount of health that the player must defeat.

UI:
There will be a main menu where the user can click a button to start the game. The gameplay will start at level 1 and go all the way until level 10. If the user fails a level, a screen will appear signaling that the user can replay the current level or click the “home” button to go back to the main menu. If the user completes a level, a screen will appear where the user can replay the current level, go back to the main menu, or move on to the next level. In the main menu, the user also has the option to choose what level they want to play.

Audio:
For the audio, there will be a small explosion sound whenever an enemy is defeated. This will occur in sync with the explosion animations that happen when the enemy disappears. This is similar to the Angry Birds game when the pigs are defeated.
